Coverage Summary

F-Prime vs. AACR 2026 Themes

F-Prime has active Box deal memo coverage across 22 CAR-T companies, with additional coverage in oncology diagnostics (Exosome Diagnostics), immunology (NaNotics, Incysus), and IO (Checkpoint Biologics, Nested Tx). The coverage profile reflects F-Prime's historical biotech focus: strong in cell therapy and diagnostics, thinner in ADC platforms, KRAS small molecules, and the newer surface antigen categories (B7-H3, DLL3, CDH17).

The gaps are most visible in three areas: ADC payload/linker platform companies, KRAS degrader programs, and spatial biology infrastructure. All three are receiving significant AACR 2026 research volume with no corresponding F-Prime coverage in Box.

Recommended Actions

Next Steps from This Analysis

Revisit Cell Therapy Box Coverage
The 22 CAR-T companies in Box were evaluated largely before AACR 2026's solid tumor CAR-T clarity. A rapid rescore against DLL3, B7-H3, and intracranial delivery angles would identify which programs are more relevant today than when first reviewed.
REFRESH
Close the ADC Gap Immediately
ADC (267 abstracts) is the fastest-growing modality and second only to bispecifics in volume — yet F-Prime has zero Box coverage in this category. This is a critical blind spot. ADC platforms with differentiated payload chemistry, linker technology, or novel target access (CDH17, B7-H3) will define the next cycle. Sourcing urgency: HIGH.
PRIORITY
Track KRAS Degrader Startups
13 KRAS degrader abstracts at AACR 2026. This is where the academic to startup translation pipeline is most active. Several programs are likely at the incubation or pre-seed stage. Identifying the university groups generating this research is the precursor to identifying investable companies before they formalize.
SCOUT
Endometrial: White Space Opportunity
Endometrial cancer is the most common gynecologic malignancy and receives 1/8th the research attention of breast cancer. ER-positive endometrial has identical biology to ER-positive breast but near-zero ADC investment. A company building focused endometrial programs using validated breast modalities (CDK4/6, FRα ADC, ESR1 degraders) owns this white space. This is a clean, high-conviction thesis.
